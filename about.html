Meet The Owner

The Hands & Heart Behind Mo's

A Marine, a Cummins Certified technician, and the person who shows up to your driveway.

Vince Morales

Owner & Lead Technician

I'm Vince Morales, the heart and hands behind Mo's Mobile Mechanic. Based in Wharton, Texas, I am dedicated to bringing expert automotive and diesel repair right to your doorstep.

After serving as an Infantry Assaultman in the U.S. Marine Corps from 2006 to 2010, where I was part of 1st Battalion 5th Marines with two deployments including one to Afghanistan, I carried the discipline, precision, and commitment I valued in the military into my work today.

From there I dove into automotive and diesel mechanics, building over a decade of experience. My journey began with formal training at UTI Houston, where I earned my Cummins Certification, and continued through work with Penske, Ford, and Mustang CAT before I opened Mo's Mobile Mechanic in 2024.
