Before You Book

Find Your VIN

Your Vehicle Identification Number lets me pull the right service manuals, quote accurate estimates, order the correct parts, and check for recalls. Here is where to find it.

Driver's Side Door

Open the driver's side door and check the door post where it latches. There is usually a label or sticker with the VIN.

Registration

Your VIN is printed on your vehicle registration document. Keep a copy somewhere safe and handy.

Insurance Card

Your insurance card or policy documents will also list the VIN, so that is another easy place to check.

Dashboard

Stand outside the vehicle and look at the corner where the dashboard meets the windshield on the driver's side.
